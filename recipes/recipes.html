Cheese Pizza

Description

This recipe is for creating a 12 inch cheese pizza

Ingredients

  • pizza crust
  • 1/2-3/4 cups of pizza sauce
  • 8 oz of sliced mozzeralla cheese
  • 1.5 cups of shreeded mozzarella cheese
  • 1-3 tb of grated parmesan cheese
  • ground basil

Steps

  1. Top the dough with pizza sauce, sliced mozzarella, shredded mozzarella, grated parmesan, and basil
  2. Bake for 12-15 minutes

Pesto Penne

Description

Simple recipe for making a yummy pasta made with penne noodles and pesto sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 large bunch of basil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup of pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup of extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 1 lb of penne

Steps

  1. Remove basil leaves from stems, and wash the basil. Then smash and peel the garlic
  2. Put basil, garlic, and pine nuts in food processor unitl coarse. Then add olive oil. Put the pesto into a bowl and stir in cheese and add salt and pepper
  3. Boil pot of water and add salt. Add penne and stir occasionally for 8-10 mins. Drain pasta and save 1/4 of water from pot. Put pasta in bowl and toss with the sauce and water with tongs

Minestrone

Description

This is a recipe for the tasty, hearty minestrone soup

Ingredients

  • 4 tb extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium chopped, yellow onion
  • 2 medium peeled, chopped carrots
  • 2 medium chopped ribs of celery
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 cups chopped seasonal vegetables
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp of dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp of dried thyme
  • 28 oz of diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 bay leaves
  • pinch of red pepper
  • ground black pepper
  • 1 cup of whole grain elbow pasta
  • 15 oz of cannelli beans rinsed and drained
  • 2 cups of baby spinach
  • 2 tsp of lemon juice
  • grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Warm the olive oil in a pot. Then add onion, carrot, celery, tomato paste, and salt. Cook, stirring often for 7-10 minutes
  2. Add the seasonal vegetables, garlic, oregano, and thyme.
  3. Pour in diced tomatoes, broth, and water. Add salt, bay leaves, red pepper, and black pepper.
  4. Raise heat to medium-high and bring to boil. Then cover with lid and reduce heat to let simmer.
  5. Cook for 15 minutes, then add in pasta, beans, and greens. Continue simmering uncovered for 20 minutes.
  6. Remove pot from heat, and remove bay leaves. Stir in lemon juice and olive oil.
